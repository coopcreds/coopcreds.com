--- title: "Test Cooperative Credentials" description: "Instructions for testing out cooperative credentials (a verifiable credential)." jquery: true ---

You can test out Cooperative Credentials right now! Follow these instructions and you'll be issued a test credential which gives you access to a private category on our forum.

  1. If you haven't already, sign up for an account on our forum.
  2. Download the MATTR wallet app through the iOS App Store or the Google Play Store.
  3. Scan the QR code below with the MATTR wallet app, or if you're on mobile tap the "Get Credential" button.
  4. After a few prompts, you'll be shown a login screen. Login with your Cooperative Credentials account (same as your forum account).
  5. Once you've got your test credential go to our forum and click the green passport in the header.

You can test out Cooperative Credentials right now! Follow these instructions and you'll be issued a test credential which gives you access to a private category on our forum.

  1. Email contact@coopcreds.com to get added to the test whitelist (you need to be a member of a participating cooperative).
  2. Download the MATTR wallet app through the iOS App Store or the Google Play Store.
  3. Scan the QR code below with the MATTR wallet app.
  4. After a few prompts, you'll be shown a login screen. Sign up with the same email that was added to the whitelist in 1.
  5. Once you've got your test credential go to the credential group in our forum and click "Verify Credentials".
